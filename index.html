About Horizons

Horizons is a travel company founded on June 25, 2026, by entrepreneur and traveler Alexander Morozov.
The company was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves an unforgettable journey. With over 15 years of experience traveling the globe,
Alexander decided to create a platform that makes tourism accessible, safe, and inspiring for everyone.

What We Do:

Horizons specializes in organizing travel routes to the most beautiful and exotic corners of the planet. We offer:

  • Personalized itineraries tailored to your interests
  • Comfortable accommodation in top hotels and unique locations
  • Experienced guides who know every corner of the destinations
  • Full travel organization, from visas to transfers
  • Affordable prices with no hidden fees
